"Cybersecurity. By Students, For Everyone"

FrigidSec(The Community) is a Student-Managed Official Cybersecurity Community of Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal. Established by Division of Cyber Security and Digital Forensics [CSDF], School of Computer Science and Engineering in Aug,2020.

Members of The Community actively participate in Capture The Flag (CTFs), Secure Coding Tournaments, Conferences, Talks and everything that says cyber.
